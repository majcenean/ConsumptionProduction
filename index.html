production & consumption

This project aims to explore production and consumption within the era of late-stage capitalism, primarily the effects thrust upon social environments, morality, resources, and the natural world. Also addressed are themes of scarcity, rapacity, and the human propensity for disastrous, bloody conflict in times of peril.
